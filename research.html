Research

In Spring 2023, I worked on a project focused on application of category theory, under the supervision of Prof. Olcay Coskun. In this project we studied the Prof. Tom Leinster's paper called Entropy and Diversity: The Axiomatic Approach. Here I learned about how to grasp physical concepts like entropy in pure algebraic ways. In Summer 2023, I started a project on Class Number Formula under the guidance of Gaurish Korpal. I am still working on this project. You can find my notes on the subject here.
